Capture from a real tab

For pages that proxy and cloud render can't reach — logged-in pages, or hard bot walls (Cloudflare/Incapsula). You capture the page in your own browser, where it's fully loaded and signed in, then load it here to preview Codi's changes against it.

  1. Set up once: drag this to your bookmarks bar → Codi Capture  
    CSP-strict site? Use the console instead: then paste it in the tab's DevTools console.
  2. On the site: open your page in another tab (sign in / pass any checks so it's fully loaded), then click the Codi Capture bookmark. It copies the page to your clipboard.
  3. Back here: paste it below and load.
